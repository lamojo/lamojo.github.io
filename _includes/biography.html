Biography

Jordan Lamotte is a Junior Systems Engineer at MyWebGrocer in Winooski, Vermont. He received his B.S. in Computer Networking & Information Security with a specialization in Cyber Security from Champlain College in May 2015; his senior capstone project compared the return-on-investment (performance, cost, and usability) of two industry-leading server virtualization suites: VMware vSphere and Microsoft Hyper-V.

Jordan has filled various roles in IT throughout his professional career. He started his career at the Milton Town School District as an IT Technician providing hardware and software technical support, network troubleshooting, and end user support. Subsequently, he was an Easy Tech Resident Technician for Staples providing computer hardware and software support, hardware repair, selling computers and printers, and providing excellent customer service. Jordan also formerly held positions in the eLearning Department at Champlain College as a Data Migration Specialist and as a Canvas Outreach Specialist where he quality checked courses being migrated between online learning management systems and provided support to faculty and staff using Canvas. Prior to being hired as a Junior Systems Engineer, Jordan completed a yearlong internship with MyWebGrocer on their Systems Support team where he facilitated computer software and hardware troubleshooting and repair, wrote documentation and tutorials to enhance and streamline the user support, and provided IT training to new employees.
